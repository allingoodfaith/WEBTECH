History with Games

My parents were a little strict about my access to the internet for a while when I was really little. I also remember playing the games that were bundled with cereal boxes like Cap’n Crunch Crunchling Adventure. As of now I think I’m best described as a Nintendo fan with heavy Indie leanings. I mostly play on Switch (happy switch 2 being announced to all that celebrate) and my PC. I’ve been chipping through Balatro and put some insance hours on Hades 2 even in early access but I wouldn’t call myself a roughlike/lite fan.
